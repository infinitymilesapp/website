Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to ∞miles ("InfinityMiles").

∞miles is a cozy home organization app designed to help individuals and families organize belongings, preserve memories, and share trusted home spaces together.

We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your information safe.

1. Information We Collect

To provide core app functionality, ∞miles may collect and store:

Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In:

  • Google account identifier
  • Display name
  • Email address

This information is used only to create and manage your account within ∞miles.

Content You Create

You may choose to create:

  • Home spaces
  • Rooms and storage locations
  • Inventory items
  • Photos
  • Memory entries
  • Home community posts
  • Shared home invitations

Only the information you choose to add is stored.

Uploaded Images

Photos uploaded through the app may be stored using cloud storage services to support item organization, memories, and home community sharing.

2. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Operate the app
  • Save and organize your home data
  • Enable shared home features
  • Display home community content you choose to share
  • Improve reliability and security
  • Restore data when you sign in again

We do not sell personal information.

We do not use your information for advertising.

3. Shared Homes

∞miles allows users to invite trusted family members or friends into a shared home.

When a home is shared:

  • Members may view information shared within that home
  • Home owners control invitations
  • Members can leave a shared home at any time

Please share homes only with people you trust.

4. Shared Home Features

∞miles allows members of a shared home to post moments, photos, and home-related updates within that shared home.

Content shared within a home is visible only to members of that home.

5. Data Storage

∞miles uses secure cloud services to store account and app data.

Depending on the feature, information may be stored using:

  • Firebase services
  • Cloudinary image storage
  • Local device storage

Data is stored only for app functionality.

6. Data Sharing

∞miles does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Information is shared only:

  • With members of homes you choose to share
  • When required by law

7. Children's Privacy

∞miles is designed to be family friendly.

Parents and guardians should supervise younger users when sharing photos, memories, or home community content.

8. Data Deletion

You may delete content you create within the app.

If you wish to request deletion of your account data, please contact us using the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

As ∞miles grows, we may update this Privacy Policy.

Updated versions will be posted within the app and through our official Privacy Policy link.

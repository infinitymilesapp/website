Delete Your ∞miles Account

Last Updated: June 2026

We're sorry to see you go. If you would like to permanently delete your ∞miles account and associated data, you can request deletion by email.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please email us at:

love.infinitymiles@gmail.com

Use this subject line:

Delete My ∞miles Account

Please include the Google Sign-In email address connected to your ∞miles account. You may also include your display name to help us locate your account.

What Will Be Deleted

When your request is completed, we will delete account data associated with your ∞miles account, which may include:

  • Account profile information used by ∞miles
  • Home spaces
  • Rooms and storage locations
  • Inventory items
  • Memory entries
  • Shared home membership information
  • Home community posts you created
  • Uploaded photos associated with your account, where applicable

What May Be Kept Temporarily

Some information may be retained temporarily if required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or service-reliability purposes. Any retained information will be kept only as long as necessary.

Deletion Timeline

Most deletion requests are completed within 30 days after we verify the account request.
